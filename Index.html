Hello, I’m Joni Lampela

About Me

I’m a multidisciplinary developer and digital artist with a unique blend of backgrounds in business information technology and social services. Professionally, I’ve worked in the gaming industry as a 2D/3D artist, while also building a strong foundation in programming and software development.

As an artist, I’m entirely self-taught, with over 15 years of experience in Blender specializing in 3D modeling and texturing. On the technical side, I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React/React Native, C#, and SQL, combining creativity with solid development skills.

Currently, I’m focused on personal projects, including a Unity VR game and a React Native mobile app that uses an API to fetch, analyze, and display data from a database.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about gaming—both video games and board games—along with art, coding, and spending time with my pets. I enjoy exploring the intersection of creativity and technology, constantly learning and experimenting with new ideas.
